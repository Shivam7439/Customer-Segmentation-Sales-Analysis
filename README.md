# Customer-Segmentation-Sales-Analysis
End-to-end business analytics project using Power BI, focusing on sales analysis, customer segmentation, and data-driven insights (2000+ dataset).
 
## Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of sales performance and customer behavior for the period 2023–2024. The objective is to derive actionable insights related to revenue trends, customer segmentation, and regional performance using interactive dashboards built in Power BI.

## Business Objectives

* Evaluate overall sales and profitability performance
* Identify high-performing products and regions
* Analyze customer behavior and purchase patterns
* Segment customers based on value contribution
* Measure customer retention and growth trends

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI (Dashboard Development & Visualization)
* Google Sheets (Data Preparation)
* DAX (Data Modeling & Calculations)

## Dashboard Highlights

* Sales Performance Analysis (Revenue, Profit, Monthly Trends)
* Customer Analysis (Segmentation, Frequency, Lifetime Value)
* Region-wise and Product-wise performance insights
* Customer Growth and Retention Analysis

## Key Insights

* Total Sales reached ₹2.54 Cr with a profit of ₹43.97 Lakh, reflecting strong business performance
* South region emerged as the leading contributor, followed by West
* T-shirt and Backpack categories generated the highest revenue
* High-value customers contributed approximately 78% of total sales and profit
* Customer acquisition trend indicates a decline, highlighting the need for improved acquisition strategies

## Dataset Information

The dataset used in this project is synthetically generated and consists of **2000+ records**, ensuring realistic and scalable analysis.
It includes key fields such as Order Date, Customer ID, Product, Sales, Profit, Region, and Customer Attributes.
